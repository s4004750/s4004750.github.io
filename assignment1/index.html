The application of paper/stop motion graphics within this particular interactive experience branches from a thoughtful consideration of user behaviour and psychology. Particularly, this decision explores the intrinsic association between paper and the duty of individuals tasked with tidying up after others. By utilising this visual motif, the interactive webpage definitely does tap into hidden cognitive relations. Using textures such as crumpled up paper shows this connection vividly. The creator frequently shows how over time people in these professions have been appreciated, such as showing and documenting artworks Janitors have specifically been in. This is done to reimagine the persona and stop the preconception we have against these people.
